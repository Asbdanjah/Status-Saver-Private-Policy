Status Saver - Privacy Policy & Permissions

Privacy Policy

Status Saver values your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All saved statuses (images and videos) remain on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

The app requires certain permissions to function properly, but these are used only for their intended purposes and not for any data collection or tracking.

Advertising: Status Saver displays ads provided by third-party networks (such as Google AdMob). These networks may use cookies or unique device identifiers to show you relevant ads, but no personal data is collected by this app.

Permissions Required

  • Photos / Media / Files access – Allows the app to read and save WhatsApp status images and videos locally on your device.
  • Storage access – Needed to store saved statuses in your phone’s gallery and ensure they remain available offline.
  • Internet access – Required only to load and display ads. The app does not send or share personal data.
  • Notification permission (Android 13+) – Used to send optional app notifications, such as updates or reminders. You can disable this permission anytime from your device settings.
  • Foreground service – Allows the app to run background operations (like monitoring new statuses) while ensuring stable performance. This does not involve collecting or transmitting personal information.

Children's Privacy

Status Saver is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take appropriate steps.
